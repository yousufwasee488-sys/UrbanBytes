Our Story

Born Street.
Built Premium.

Urban Bites started with one obsession — making the most satisfying burger you've ever had. The kind that's dripping, stacked and impossible to put down.

Street food at heart. No white tablecloths, no pretension. Just real ingredients, bold recipes, and maximum flavour. Every patty is hand-pressed. Every bun toasted. Every sauce made in-house, daily.

We cut out the middleman — no apps, no commissions. Direct to you, fast and fresh.
