Our Story

Born Street.
Built Premium.

Urban Bites started with a single obsession — making the most satisfying burger you've ever had. The kind that's stacked, dripping, and impossible to put down.

We're street food at heart. No white tablecloths, no pretension. Just real ingredients, bold recipes, and maximum flavour. Every patty is hand-pressed and smashed to order. Every bun toasted. Every sauce made in-house.

We cut out the middleman — direct orders, delivered fast and fresh to your door.
